H. Benyamin Sueb

Born in Batavia on March 5, 1939

Died in Jakarta on September 5, 1995

Awards

The 1973 Citra Trophy in the film Intan Berduri (Turino Djunaidy, 1972) with Rima Melati.

Citra Cup 1975 in the film Si Doel Anak Modern (Sjuman Djaya, 1975).

The Kemayoran Runway was changed to its name.

The Parama Dharma Culture Star Award from President Susilo Bambang Yudhoyono, at the State Palace, Jakarta, Tuesday, November 8, 2011.
